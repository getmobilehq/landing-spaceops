The status quo

Running cleaning operations shouldn't feel like this.

01

No visibility

You don't know if your team showed up, which rooms were cleaned, or whether quality standards were met — until a client complains.

02

Manual everything

You're tracking attendance on paper, scheduling on WhatsApp, and building reports in Excel. Every week, hours disappear into admin work.

03

Client trust gap

Clients ask for proof of service and you scramble to put together something presentable. There's no system of record, just word of mouth.

The platform

One platform. Complete control.

SpaceOPS connects every role in your operation — from the janitor on the floor to the client in the boardroom — into a single, real-time workflow.

01 — PLAN

Plan & schedule

Create cleaning activities, assign rooms, and set recurring schedules. Templates replicate proven routines across buildings in seconds.

02 — EXECUTE

Execute & track

Janitors clock in with GPS-verified attendance, work through assigned tasks, and flag issues on the spot. Always know who's where.

03 — INSPECT

Inspect & score

Supervisors scan rooms, run inspections against custom checklists, and record pass/fail. Every room gets a quality score.

04 — PROVE

Report & prove

Generate polished PDFs with AI-written executive summaries, performance charts, and issue breakdowns. Send them to clients.

Capabilities

Everything you need to run a best-in-class operation.

Buildings & floor plans

Add buildings, define floors and rooms, upload floor plans, and let AI detect room boundaries automatically.

Smart scheduling

Daily, weekly, or monthly recurring templates. Assign janitors to specific rooms with a few clicks.

GPS-verified attendance

Geofenced clock-in. No buddy punching, no guesswork. Know exactly when staff arrived and where.

Quality inspections

QR-scan rooms, run custom checklists, record results instantly. Pass rates calculate automatically.

Issue tracking

Report deficiencies with photos, severity levels, and precise room locations. SLA timers track resolution.

AI-powered reports

One-click PDFs with AI-written executive summaries. Charts show trends by building, floor, team, client.

Client portal

Read-only dashboards for clients. SLA compliance, activity history, pass rates — transparency builds trust.

Payroll management

Hourly rates, overtime rules, automatic calculation from clock-ins. Approve and export to CSV.
